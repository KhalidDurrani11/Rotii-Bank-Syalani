About Saylani Roti Bank

A beacon of hope in the fight against hunger

Our Mission

Saylani Roti Bank is a noble initiative by Saylani Welfare International Trust, dedicated to providing free meals to the underprivileged and needy families across Pakistan. We believe that no one should go to bed hungry, and through our network of volunteers and donors, we work tirelessly to ensure food reaches those who need it most.

Our Vision

To create a hunger-free Pakistan where every family has access to nutritious meals and no child sleeps hungry. We envision a society where compassion and generosity unite to eliminate food insecurity.
